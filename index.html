W Ø F WOODEN

is a furniture company and design studio with a minimalist aesthetic that emphasizes functionality and is heavily influenced by Asian and local culture. All products are designed to be flat-packs with personality and always deliver a distinct story.

PRODUCTS

KIHON

Kihon means basic. And basic is a fundamental form of something. When the decorative and ornamental shapes are removed, the distillation of form emerges. Kihon is our learning process to give chair & stool a character without burdening it with unnecessary elements.

SHI

Every piece, while simple, had a function and elegance that helped enhance the living area of the family it belonged to. Shi Chair is made to be comfortable and versatile for various area and placements. The minimalist impression hides its own complexity in some of the wood joints. The floating seat is also well-executed on this chair.

Project @Stoffel.id

Being one of the hidden gem cafe in Batu City, Stoffel presents a concept with neat buildings and furniture. Home-style serving, slow bar and being close to customers. This turntable shelf becomes an attractive point between the front of the bar area and the Stoffel entrance
